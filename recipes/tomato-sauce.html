Tomato & basil sauce recipe

Tomato & basil sauce served with spaghetti

Save time and money with this simple and tasty sauce, great with pasta, meat or fish

Ingredients
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp vegetable stock powder or ½ crumbled stock cube
  • 1 tbsp tomato purée
  • few basil leaves
Steps
  1. Heat the olive oil in a pan, add the garlic clove, then gently fry for 1 min.
  2. Tip in the chopped tomatoes, vegetable stock powder, tomato purée and sugar, then bring to the boil. Reduce the heat, then simmer uncovered for 5 mins, stirring occasionally.
  3. To finish, tear a few basil leaves, then stir into the sauce.
